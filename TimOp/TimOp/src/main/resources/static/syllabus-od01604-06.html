교과목
개요		 1. Develop students’ global ability in the fundamentals of English communication with an emphasis on reading and writing. 2. Develop students’ leadership ability through evaluating and synthesizing information will improve their English written communication skills through interpersonal activities and lessons. 3. Make logical inferences and critical thinking based on multi-cultural materials.
교과목
운영		 수업목표 The goal of this course is to develop students’ abilities in the fundamentals of English communication with an emphasis on listening and speaking. This will provide the building blocks for more complex and advanced communicative English skills. Upon completion of the course, students will be able to use the narrative present tense, simple past tense, future tense with ‘going to’, use modal verbs correctly, ask for clarification and repetition, present information from notes, introduce topics in a presentation, and make notes from a word web.
교수학습
세부운영
방법		 Lecture, activities, in-class activities such as peer-to-peer speaking, micro and macro listening comprehension, audio prediction and analysis, identifying main ideas and vocabulary development, as well as developing interpersonal skills in the target language.
